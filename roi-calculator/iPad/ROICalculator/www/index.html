Area Monitoring
Description:
Are all vehicles where they should be at the right time? The Area Monitoring app offers a simple and effective option for vehicle monitoring. Fleet operators are informed via the Web portal when vehicles or machines leave or enter certain areas, the carrier’s grounds, construction sites, etc. The information can be sent as an alert via email or SMS. The vehicle-specific monitoring areas can be defined as circles, squares, or polygons.

Specific benefit:
  • The operator can monitor vehicles and machines.
  • The operator can monitor predetermined areas.
  • The operator can monitor the positions of the vehicles.
  • Theft protection for vehicles and machines improves.
  • The operator is informed about departure and arrival times of the vehicles.

Order Fulfillment Basic
Description:
Good fleet management goes hand in hand with rapid, clear communication between the transport company and the vehicles on the road. The Order Fulfillment Basic app supports this. On the one hand, it allows driving assignments or service notifications to be transferred from the portal to the display in the vehicle; in the process, new destinations are sent directly to the navigation system. On the other hand, the app generates status notifications for the current assignments and sends them back to the portal, where they are displayed in a table or on a map.

Specific benefit:
  • Assignments and destinations are communicated rapidly and directly.
  • Communications costs are reduced; communication errors are avoided.
  • The employer gets a better overview of the logistic processes.

Truck Navigation
Description:
A navigation system for trucks has to be able to do more than show a generic fastest or shortest route to the destination. It has to calculate the best route in relation to the vehicle – like the Truck Navigation app does. Therefore the vehicles' dimensions, weights, and hazardous materials categories can be entered into the navigation app and stored in profiles. These are taken into account for routing, as is information about environmental zones, for instance.

Specific benefit:
  • Professional routing saves times and mileage.
  • Possible fines for driving through closed areas and streets are avoided.
Zahlungsweise:
monatlich
Truck Navigation
Beschreibung:
Ein Navigationssystem für Lkw muss mehr können, als den allgemein schnellsten oder kürzesten Weg zum Ziel zu zeigen. Es muss – wie die App Truck Navigation – fahrzeugbezogen die beste Route berechnen. In der Navigationsapp lassen sich daher Maße, Gewichte und Gefahrgutklassen der Fahrzeuge eingeben und in Profilen speichern. Diese werden beim jeweiligen Routing ebenso berücksichtigt wie beispielsweise Angaben zu Umweltzonen.

Konkreter Nutzen:

Vehicles Overview
Description:
With Vehicles Overview one can easily track the current location of a vehicle via the web portal. The app shows the position of one vehicle, several vehicles, or a whole fleet on a map.

  • The position of vehicles for passenger transport can be determined at any time.
  • The optional representation of the vehicle by type or number simplifies visual evaluation of the maps.

DigiTacho Download & D8 Info
Description:
The digital tachograph gathers the drivers' driving and rest times in commercial vehicles. Employers are required by law to cull and store these data regularly. The Digitacho-Download app takes this job off your hands. It downloads the data from the mass storage and the driver card completely fully automatically according to the legally required cycles. The App visualizes the data in Openmatics portal and makes them ready for archiving in the appropriate file format.

Specific benefit:
  • The fully automatic download does away with the time-consuming manual readout of the data.
  • The readout cycles required by law are reliably adhered to.
  • The download is possible worldwide, independent of the current position of the vehicle.
  • The data display allows for rapid inspection and analysis.

monatlich

Pre-Trip-Inspection
Description:
Vehicles are generally inspected before they leave the garage. Until now, the drivers or garage employees had to document the results manually. The Pre-Trip-Inspection app now allows electronic data gathering via any mobile device with a web browser. The data gathered are forwarded directly to the Openmatics Portal and assigned to the relevant vehicle. Operators can define pre-inspection checklists themselves and specifically apply them to individual vehicles in the fleet according to type, technology, and need.

Specific benefit:
  • Correct inspection of the vehicles is ensured.
  • The operator can follow the inspections easily and check them thoroughly.
  • The administrative workload is reduced.
    (The operator is provided with simple tracing and control means.)
